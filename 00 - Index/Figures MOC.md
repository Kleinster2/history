# Historical Figures MOC

People important for what they did: rulers, reformers, financiers, industrialists, soldiers, fossils, founders, fraudsters, and other historical actors who changed economic or institutional outcomes.

Use [[Thinkers MOC]] for people primarily included because their ideas, methods, or interpretations shape how the vault reads history.

---

## All Figures

```dataview
TABLE period, category
FROM "Figures"
WHERE type = "person"
SORT period ASC, file.name ASC
```

## Economic Significance

The vault emphasizes figures who changed how economies work, not just who ruled.

| Type | Examples | Why They Matter |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| Law-givers | [[Hammurabi]] | Created institutional frameworks |
| Trade builders | [[Hatshepsut]], [[Ahmed Zaki Yamani]] | Expanded or redirected exchange networks |
| System creators | [[Sargon of Akkad]], [[John D. Rockefeller]] | Unified political or economic spaces |
| Industrial organizers | [[Andrew Carnegie]], [[Henry Clay Frick]] | Changed production scale and labor relations |
| Financial operators | [[James Stillman]], [[George Herbert Walker]] | Built capital-market and banking structures |
| Fraud cases | [[Charles Ponzi]], [[Bernie Madoff]] | Reveal trust, regulation, and market failure |
