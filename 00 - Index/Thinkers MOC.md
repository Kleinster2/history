# Thinkers MOC

People important for the frameworks, methods, and interpretations they give the vault. A thinker note is less biography than intellectual infrastructure: core thesis, key works, where the vault uses the idea, and the strongest critiques.

Use [[Figures MOC]] for people primarily included because of what they did inside history.

---

## All Thinkers

```dataview
TABLE period, category
FROM "Thinkers"
WHERE type = "person"
SORT period ASC, file.name ASC
```
