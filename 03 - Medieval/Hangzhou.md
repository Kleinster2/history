# Hangzhou

Hangzhou was the largest city in human history during the Southern Song dynastyâ€”home to over one million people in a commercial economy unmatched until the Industrial Revolution. Marco Polo called it "the finest and most splendid city in the world." It represented the peak of pre-modern urban civilization and commercial development.
