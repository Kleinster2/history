# Hammurabi

Hammurabi was the sixth king of the First Babylonian Dynasty, reigning from 1792 to 1750 BCE. Through diplomacy and military conquest, he transformed Babylon from a minor city-state into the dominant power in Mesopotamia. He is best known for his law code, one of the oldest and most complete legal documents from the ancient world.
