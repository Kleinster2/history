# Cleopatra VII

Cleopatra VII Philopator was the last active ruler of Ptolemaic Egypt and one of history's most famous women. A skilled diplomat, linguist, and administrator, she navigated the dangerous waters of Roman politics while managing Egypt's vast economy. Her relationships with Julius Caesar and Mark Antony were as much strategic economic alliances as personal liaisons, aimed at preserving Egyptian independence and her dynasty's wealth.
