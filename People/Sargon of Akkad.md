# Sargon of Akkad

Sargon of Akkad (Akkadian: *Šarru-kīn*, "the true king") was the founder of the Akkadian Empire, history's first multi-ethnic empire. Rising from obscure origins to conquer all of Mesopotamia and beyond, Sargon became a legendary figure whose story inspired rulers for millennia.
