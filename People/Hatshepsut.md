# Hatshepsut

Hatshepsut was one of ancient Egypt's most successful pharaohs and the most powerful woman to rule Egypt before Cleopatra. Reigning for approximately 21 years during the 18th Dynasty, she oversaw an era of unprecedented prosperity, monumental building, and expanded trade networks. Her famous expedition to Punt revitalized Egyptian commerce, while her construction projects employed thousands and stimulated the economy.
