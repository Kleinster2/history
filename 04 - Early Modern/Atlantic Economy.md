# Atlantic Economy

The Atlantic economy was the engine of the Great Divergence—a system of trade connecting Europe, Africa, and the Americas that enriched European powers, devastated Africa, and built the Americas on enslaved labor. Understanding it is essential for understanding how the modern global economy emerged, and why some regions developed while others were impoverished.
