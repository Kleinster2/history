# Food History

Food—the most fundamental of human needs—has shaped economies, cultures, and civilizations. From hunting and gathering to industrial agriculture, how societies produce, distribute, and consume food reveals essential economic patterns. Food history illuminates agricultural development, trade networks, labor systems, and living standards across time.
