# Money History

Money—the medium of exchange, store of value, and unit of account—evolved from commodity currencies through precious metals to paper money and now digital forms. Each monetary innovation transformed economic possibilities, enabling larger-scale trade, more complex economies, and new financial instruments. Understanding money's history illuminates how this fundamental institution shapes economic life.
