# Modern History (1800 - Present)

The Modern period witnessed the most dramatic economic transformation in human history. Industrialization spread from Britain to the world, living standards rose exponentially for billions, and the global economy became truly integrated. This era saw the end of empires, two world wars, the rise and fall of communism, and the emergence of mass prosperity—alongside unprecedented inequality and environmental challenges.
