# Augustus

Augustus (63 BCE - 14 CE), Rome's first emperor, transformed the chaotic economy of the late Republic into a stable system that would sustain the empire for centuries. His currency reforms, provincial reorganization, and establishment of the Pax Romana created the conditions for the ancient world's greatest economic expansion.
