# Hieroglyphics

Egyptian hieroglyphics ("sacred carvings" in Greek) were the formal writing system of ancient Egypt, used for over 3,500 years. Combining logographic, alphabetic, and determinative elements, hieroglyphics were employed primarily for monumental inscriptions, religious texts, and formal documents, while cursive scripts served everyday purposes.

## Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Name | "Sacred carved letters" (Greek) |
