# Lothal

Lothal was a vital trade center of the Indus Valley Civilization, located in Gujarat near the Gulf of Khambhat. Famous for its possible dockyard—potentially the world's oldest—Lothal provides crucial evidence for Harappan maritime trade and economic organization.

## Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Location | Gujarat, India |
| Water access | Sabarmati River, Gulf of Khambhat |
| Period | c. 2400-1900 BCE |

| Discovery | 1954 |
