# Pompeii

Pompeii, destroyed by Vesuvius in 79 CE, provides the most detailed snapshot of Roman urban economy ever preserved. Frozen in time, its shops, workshops, taverns, and houses reveal the daily economic life that other sites only hint at—prices painted on walls, goods in storage, coins in purses, the working city caught mid-transaction.

## Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Location | Bay of Naples, Italy |
| Population | ~11,000 (estimated) |
| Destruction | August 24, 79 CE |
| Preservation | Volcanic ash sealed city |
| Discovery | 1748 |
| Significance | Best-preserved Roman city |
