# Anyang (Yinxu)

Anyang, known archaeologically as Yinxu ("Ruins of Yin"), was the last capital of the Shang dynasty and the birthplace of Chinese historical civilization. Here archaeologists found the oracle bones that proved the Shang's existence and the royal tombs that revealed the staggering wealth—and brutal sacrifices—of China's Bronze Age elite economy.
