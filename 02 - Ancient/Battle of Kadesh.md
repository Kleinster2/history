# Battle of Kadesh

The Battle of Kadesh (1274 BCE) was the ancient world's largest chariot battleâ€”Egyptians vs. Hittites fighting for control of Syria. Neither side won decisively, though both claimed victory. The real significance came after: the world's first recorded peace treaty between Ramesses II and Hattusili III. Kadesh demonstrates how Bronze Age powers balanced war and diplomacy.
