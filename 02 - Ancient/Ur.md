# Ur

Ur was one of the most important cities of ancient Mesopotamia, serving as a major Sumerian city-state and later the capital of the Ur III dynasty (2112-2004 BCE). Famous for its massive ziggurat, the Royal Tombs with their spectacular grave goods, and its association with the biblical Abraham, Ur remains one of the best-preserved and most extensively excavated Mesopotamian sites.
