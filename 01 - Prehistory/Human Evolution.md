# Human Evolution

Human evolution—the biological and cultural development of our species—spans seven million years from the divergence from apes to modern Homo sapiens. This history involves multiple hominin species, African origins, global dispersal, and the emergence of traits that define humanity: bipedalism, tool use, large brains, language, and culture. Understanding human evolution provides context for all subsequent human history.

|---------|-------------|
