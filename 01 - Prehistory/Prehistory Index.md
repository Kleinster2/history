# Prehistory Index

The earliest chapter of human history, spanning from the deep evolutionary past through the emergence of anatomically modern humans and the development of complex societies before writing.

## Overview

Prehistory encompasses the vast majority of human existence - from the divergence of the human lineage (~7 million years ago) through the invention of writing (~3000 BCE). This period saw the evolution of bipedalism, the expansion of the human brain, the development of tools, and the global spread of our species.
