# Holocene Epoch

The Holocene Epoch—the current geological epoch beginning 11,700 years ago—provided the stable, warm climate that enabled the [[Agricultural Revolution]] and [[Rise of Civilization]]. Following the [[Ice Age]] (Pleistocene), the Holocene's relatively stable conditions allowed human societies to develop agriculture, build cities, and create complex civilizations. Some scientists argue we have now entered the "Anthropocene"—an era of human-dominated planetary change.
